1. John and the New Friend

John is playing in the park when he notices a little girl sitting alone on a bench. He gathers his courage and walks over to ask if she wants to play. The little girl smiles and says yes.

2. Learning to Share

John and his new friend are playing with a ball. When the ball rolls far away, his friend wants to go fetch it first, but John decides to give the ball to her and waits.

3. Cleaning the Park Together

John and his new friend notice some litter in the park. They decide to pick up the trash together and clean up the park.

4. Helping the Lost Cat

While walking in the park, John and his friend find a lost cat. They take the cat and try to find its owner.

5. Facing Fears

One day, John hears strange noises at night and feels scared. His mom tells him that courage isn’t about not being afraid, but about knowing how to face fear.

6. Working Together

John and his friend decide to build a big block castle. They divide the work—one builds, the other makes sure it’s stable.

7. Listening to Others' Opinions

John and his friend are arguing about how to play a game. They listen to each other's opinions and together decide on the best way to play.

8. Being Thankful Every Day

John’s mom reminds him that he should be grateful for the people and things around him every day. John starts to thank everyone who helps him and appreciates every happy moment.

9. Admitting Mistakes

John accidentally breaks his friend's toy during a game. He honestly admits his mistake and apologizes.

10. Love and Friendship

At the end of the story, John and all of his friends celebrate their day together, sharing laughter and joy. They make a wish that they will always support each other in the future.
